Our Values

At Global Go Exim, we source products directly from trusted artisanal producers across India and leverage expertise and partnerships to showcase the quality of Indian offerings abroad. We are dedicated to strict quality control and streamlined logistics to deliver excellence.

Vision

To raise the standard of indigenous products in the global markets by exporting to the ideal place for their optimum use. Solely built on integrity and ensuring that the resources are utilized to the maximum potential and transported with the utmost quality.

Mission

We assure absolute quality by exporting handpicked organic products directly from our farms, ensuring maximum resource utilization. Committed to timely delivery, we meticulously store and package our goods to preserve their exceptional quality throughout the supply chain.

Our Products

Coconut

Plucked fresh from tropical trees as natural goodness is sealed inside. Whole raw coconuts packed with nourishing coconut water and soft meat. Our mature, flawless coconuts retain maximum nutrients. Crack open to access electrolyte-rich water, and delicious soft meat and scoop out fresh chunks to uplift breakfasts, baked goods, and snacks. Let the subtle tropical flavors and creamy goodness boost health and delight taste buds. Raw coconut superfruit is nutrient-rich and versatile for everyday enjoyment. Crack, scoop, blend, and bake - easy wholesome goodness from natural coconuts. From our farm to your kitchen!

Desi Ghee

Pure. Traditional. Natural. Made from fresh cream by small dairy farmers, our handcrafted desi ghee celebrates classic techniques. Slow-simmered to perfection, with an aromatic nutty flavor, rich texture, and smooth creamy taste. Ideal for cooking, spreading, or carrying on family traditions. Pasture-grazed cows and time-honored methods are now in convenient stay-fresh packs. Fit for special occasions yet everyday nurturing. Spread ancient goodness over warm parathas or add a touch of heritage to your meals. Authentic, wholesome desi ghee, crafted for conscious kitchens.

Jaggery powder

Our jaggery powder is golden goodness crafted from pure sugarcane juice. Sun-dried and milled into fine nutritious powder with an Intense complexity of flavors - from caramel sweetness to subtle spice. Our traditional non-centrifugal cane sugar is packed with minerals and the molasses-rich powder dissolves easily into beverages and batters, sprinkled over fruit, yogurt, or cereal as it adds crunch. Our jaggery elevates health with iron, magnesium, and potassium. Experience sugar in its most wholesome, nourishing form. Versatile, micronutrient-rich cane sugar powder for conscious kitchens. Tradition and wellness in one irresistible golden superfood.

Turmeric

Sun-dried heirloom turmeric roots are slowly milled into vibrant orange powder with a potent signature aroma and bold flavor with a subtle earthy kick. A treasure trove of curcuminoids, antioxidants, and anti-inflammatory compounds with versatile super-spice that uplifts stews, curries, rice, roasted veggies, and more. Golden powder turns milk into a healing tonic and adds color and flair to dishes. Our organic turmeric boosts immunity, vitality, and overall health with nature’s multi-tasking wonder and retains maximum freshness and nutrient density.

Honey

Liquid gold from flower nectar is nature's sweet gift. Our raw, unfiltered honey brims with wholesome goodness as it provides a smooth, delicious taste and soothes sore throats, and lifts tea. Antioxidant-rich with antimicrobial, and anti-inflammatory powers with is sustainably sourced from local hives, extracted in small batches. Enjoy our honeycomb chunks melted into the warm, golden syrup. Feed your inner glow and boost immunity with bees’ handiwork. Honey’s versatile sweetness complements cheese, yogurt, and fruits galore. Put a spoonful of sunshine on your table from healthy, happy hives.
